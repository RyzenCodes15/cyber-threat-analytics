# Data Dictionary

## Destination Port
Network service port receiving traffic.

Examples:
- 80: HTTP
- 443: HTTPS
- 22: SSH

---

## Flow Duration
Time duration of a network flow.

Unit:
Microseconds.

---

## Total Fwd Packets
Packets sent from source to destination.

---

## Total Backward Packets
Packets sent from destination back to source.

---

## Flow Bytes/s
Bytes transferred per second.

---

## Label
Traffic category.

Examples:
- BENIGN
- DDoS
- PortScan
- DoS Hulk
- Bot
